Clayton Schoeny

Hello, I'm Clayton.

I am a Product Data Scientist working at Cash App on the Moneybot team. I'm happy to be on a team that is directly furthering Cash App's mission of expanding financial access and inclusion. I previously worked on the Cash Card and Cash Families teams.

I'm a Los Angeles local, born and raised in Redondo Beach. I received my BS, MS, and PhD degrees in Electrical Engineering from UCLA. My doctoral research focused on utilizing both the inherent redundancy in data and the asymmetric errors in specific channels to store, analyze, and transmit data efficiently.

I find data science to be a perfect post-academic career as it allows me to combine my strong mathematical background with programming, statistics, machine learning, and business acumen to make excellent critical decisions.
